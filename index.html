Hello! My name is Rushikesh Anil Patil. I graduated from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Pune with an Integrated BS-MS degree in May 2022. I am interested in Theoretical Physics in particular Condensed Matter Theory. So I like to study the emergant phenomena arising in strongly correlated systems. The intersection of Condensed Matter Theory, Quantum Field Theory and Quantum Information Theory fascinates me the most! Here is my Google Scholar profile.
